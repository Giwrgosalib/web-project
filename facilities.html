FACILITIES

6 Courts:

  • 3 Clay Courts
  • 2 Grass Courts
  • 1 Hard Court

All our courts are high quality and are maintained by trained specialists. Every member can reserve a court to practise or play a friendly game with other members.

Our clubhouse is a great place to relax after a match or a training session. It has a bar, a restaurant and a terrace. It is also a great place to meet other members and make new friends.

Locker Rooms:

Our locker rooms are clean and spacious. They have showers, toilets and changing rooms. They are also equipped with hairdryers and hair straighteners.

Equipment Store:

Our equipment store is located in the clubhouse. It has a wide range of tennis equipment and accessories. You can find everything you need to play tennis, from rackets to balls, from shoes to clothes. It also has a tennis ball machine and a tennis ball dispenser.Our staff is always ready to help you find the right equipment for you.

Parking:

Our parking is free and it is located right next to the club. It is also very safe and it is guarded 24/7.

Services

Tennis Lessons:

Our tennis club offers tennis lessons for all ages and levels. Our tennis club has 5 coaches. They are highly qualified and they have a lot of experience. They will help you improve your game and they will teach you the basics of tennis. You can also book private lessons if you want to learn more about tennis.

Court Reservation:

Our tennis club offers court reservation for all members. You can reserve a court for a friendly game or for a training session. You can also reserve a court for a tennis lesson. You can reserve a court for a maximum of 1.5 hours so they could be prepared and maintained for other players. You can reserve a court up to 7 days in advance.

Tournament:

Our tennis club organizes a tournament every year. It is open to all members. The tournament is held in the summer. It is a great opportunity to meet other members and to play tennis with them. The tournament is also a great opportunity to improve your game.

Member Discounts:

Our tennis club offers discounts to all members. You can get discounts on tennis equipment and accessories. You can also get discounts on tennis lessons and court reservation.
